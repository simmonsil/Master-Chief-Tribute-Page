Master Chief Petty Officer John-117

The savior of humanity

Here is a summary of the Master Chief's career:

  • 2525 - John-117 completes his first mission and is awarded a Purple Heart along with having his first encounter with the Covenant
  • September 2552 - The Master Chief participates in the battle of the first Halo ring and manages to foil the Covenant and destroy the ring before it could wipe out all life in the galaxy
  • October 2552 - Master Chief participates in the Battle of Earth, driving back the Covenant forces from our home
  • November 2, 2552 - A new Halo ring is found and the Master Chief along with a small UNSC force stop the Covenant from firing the ring. The Master Chief is also able to kill one of the Covenant Prophets, crippling their leadership
  • November 17, 2552 - The UNSC, Elites, and the Master Chief participate in the final ground engagement with the Covenant on Earth. They also manage to stop Earth from being taken over by the Flood
  • December 2552 - The Master Chief engages the Covenant during the Battle of the Ark and defeats the Covenant. He saves humanity by stopping the Ark from firing all of the Halo rings

“He is neither the smartest, nor the fastest, nor the strongest of the Spartans. But he is the bravest, and quite possibly the luckiest. And in my opinion, he is the best.”

-- Dr. Catherine Halsey
